

#Catalytix

Accelerating Molecular Discovery with AI

Overview

Catalytix is an AI-powered research platform designed to accelerate the discovery and optimization of chemical catalysts, enzymes, and metabolic pathways for sustainable fuel and chemical production.
Traditional research in catalysis and synthetic biology relies heavily on trial-and-error experimentation, which is slow, expensive, and inefficient. Catalytix addresses this challenge by combining generative AI, predictive modeling, and feedback learning to assist scientists in identifying high-performance molecular designs faster.

Problem Statement


Catalyst and enzyme discovery is time-consuming and resource-intensive


Large design space makes manual exploration inefficient


Knowledge is fragmented across research papers and experiments


Lack of data-driven decision-making in molecular design



Our Solution
Catalytix provides an end-to-end AI-driven discovery pipeline:

Retrieve known catalysts/enzymes from scientific datasets


Generate novel molecular designs using AI


Predict performance (activity, selectivity, stability)


Rank and recommend top candidates


Enable experimental validation

Learn from feedback to continuously improve models



⚙️ Key Features


Generative AI for Molecular Design
Proposes new catalyst and enzyme structures beyond existing databases


Predictive Modeling
Estimates reaction efficiency, stability, and selectivity


Reaction & Pathway Simulation
Models chemical reactions and metabolic pathways


Interactive Visualization
Molecular structures, energy diagrams, and performance plots


Feedback Learning Loop
Continuously improves using experimental results
